Association Dues and Donations

Your contribution, whether in the form of dues or a one-time donation, enables the alumni association to support the membership of the Gamma Upsilon chapter by helping with costs like Regional Leadership Conferences and Conclave, among others.

A sustaining, $5 monthly donation in the form of alumni association dues is the best way to support the association and the chapter, but you can also make a one-time donation. Whichever option you choose, know that your contribution is making a difference.

Dues

Dues are $5/month, and the buttons below will enable you to use your PayPal account or credit/debit card to sign up for a recurring $5/month payment. You are able to cancel your dues at any time through PayPal.
